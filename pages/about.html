About Us

We are a student-driven organization focused on introducing students at the University of Washington to mobile development.

What do we do?
We teach each other to develop mobile apps! Every Thursday, we give a presentation on some portion of a mobile framework. Tuesdays, more expierenced members are available during meeting times to answer questions.
What frameworks do we use?
Although we have given presentations covering other frameworks, we focus on Flutter and SwiftUI. If you are interested in learning another framework we encourage you to learn it and share what you have learned with the club! Propose a presentation on a framework through our Discord!
Where can I access the source code of projects related to the club?
Go to our GitHub repository! This repository has the code used in our presentations, our website, and more! If you are interested in contributing, pull requests are welcome.
